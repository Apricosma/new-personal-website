A Little About Me

Nice to meet you, I'm Apricosma, or Apricot, born and raised in Canada! I'm a full-stack developer currently enrolled in college for software developent, and eager to learn more.

Creative, and tech-minded, I have a strong sense for design and a good eyeball for detail. I like to push unique, striking design while still adhering to current trends and standards. I firmly believe in standing out to be remembered, but not so much that the product seems desperate for attention.

Coffee addict, life-long web surfer, and cyber/futuristic design enthusiast. Yep, I'm a 90/00s kid. I grew up on gaming and anime from a young age, and bring a modern perspective on budding trends with younger audiences.
